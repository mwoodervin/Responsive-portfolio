About Me

Mary brings 20+ experience in both the public and private sectors to her passion for education. She is a talented writer, an insightful and compassionate advocate and a critical thinker with a background in municipal finance. She provides support in all phases of the college search and application process.

Mary has served on a number of community and educational boards, most recently the board of trustees at Berwick Academy in South Berwick, Maine. She earned her B.A. at Hamilton College and her M.B.A. at the University at Albany. She and her two sons live in Stratham, NH with their adorable (if mischievous) Jack Russell Terrier, Bogie.
