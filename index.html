About Me

Mary W. Ervin is an independent consultant specializing in small business development, particularly management and systems improvement. She has a background in finance and has worked in both the private and public sectors. She is currently studying full-stack web development.

Prior to becoming an independent consultant, Mary worked in municipal finance for Lamont Financial Services, a New Jersey firm providing financial advising and data analysis for states and state agencies issuing bonds for infrastructure projects. She also spent over 10 years working in various facets of human services, including advocating for children in the foster care and adoption system in New York City; running a community residence for adults with mental health and addiction issues; and co-directing an employment project for adults with developmental disabilities.

Mary has served on several school and community boards. She served as Treasurer on the Board at the Deerfield Cooperative Preschool and on both the parent board and board of Trustees at Berwick Academy an independent school in South Berwick, Maine. She was a founding board member of Catamount Womenaid, a community-based organization dedicated to providing short-term grant funding for families in need.

Mary graduated from Magna Cum Laude from Hamilton College with a major in French and holds an MBA from the University at Albany. She and her two sons live in Stratham, NH.
