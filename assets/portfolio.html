Flick the Deck

TWhat if choosing a movie on Friday night were as effortless as picking a card ... any card? Flick-the-Deck is a lighthearted application that combines the ease of picking a card with user input to generate movie suggestions. All you need to do is click to shuffle, choose max runtime, rating and genre ... the rest is luck of the draw!
